What it does

Built to sound like you, not a bot.

Four pieces make up the assistant — each one editable from the dashboard, none of it hardcoded.

01

QR Pairing

Scan once to link your WhatsApp account through whatsapp-web.js — no separate number needed.

02

Style Matching

Trained on your own message history so tone, slang, and punctuation habits carry through.

03

Editable Prompt

Change personality, formality, or language mix any time — no redeploy required.

04

Whitelist Control

Choose exactly which chats the assistant is allowed to reply in, and flip it off instantly.

Get started

Three steps to your first auto-reply.

1

Install the APK

Download above, allow "install from unknown sources" if prompted, then open the app.

2

Scan the QR

Open WhatsApp → Linked Devices → scan the code shown in the app to pair your account.

3

Set your whitelist

Pick which chats the bot can reply in, tweak the prompt if you want, and toggle it on.
